What is an SME?

A Subject Matter Expert profile is a structured persona — a discipline, a seniority, a way of reasoning, known biases, trusted and distrusted sources, strengths and blind spots. Agents load one to think like a seasoned specialist, and can convene several to pressure-test a decision from genuinely different vantage points.

🧠
